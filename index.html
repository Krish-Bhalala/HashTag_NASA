Our Mission

Team Cosmic Explorers is dedicated to advancing our understanding of solar phenomena through cutting-edge observation and analysis of solar flares. Our mission in May 2024 aims to capture unprecedented data on solar activity, contributing to space weather prediction and furthering our knowledge of our star, the Sun.
